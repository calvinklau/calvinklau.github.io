Winner's Bag:

Justin Rose's Honma gear at the Farmers Insurance Open

Justin Rose won the Farmers Insurance Open at Torrey Pines by shooting a pair of 69s over the weekend for a 21-under total, besting second-place finisher Adam Scott by two. The Englishman recently announced his new deal with Honma, and it paid off as Rose not only grabbed his 10th career PGA Tour victory but also took home a check for $1,278,000 and 500 FedEx Cup points. Here's a full list of gear Rose used to get the job done:

Driver: Honma Tour World TW747 460 (9.5°) with Honma Vizard FD-7X shaft
Fairway Wood: TaylorMade M6 (15°) with Mitsubishi Tensei CK Orange shaft
Irons: Honma Tour World TW-X proto (2), Honma Tour World Rose proto (4-9) with KBS Tour C-Taper shafts
Wedges: Honma Rose proto (47°, 52°, 56°), Titleist Vokey SM7 Raw (60°), with KBS Hi-Rev 2.0 shafts
Putter: Axis prototype
Ball: TaylorMade TP5
